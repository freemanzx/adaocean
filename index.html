State of the Art
Bare Metal Stake Pool Cluster

Our high-availability stake pool cluster boasts multiple built-in redundancies, ensuring optimal performance at all times. Each node is deployed on a separate bare metal server housed in data centers, with five relays utilizing low-latency connections to guarantee efficient block propagation.

Around the Clock Monitoring
by Industry Experts

Our comprehensive telemetry monitoring is achieved through the use of professional-grade tools and mobile phone event notifications. To ensure uninterrupted block production, we maintain multiple redundant nodes, poised to take over automatically if needed.

Nodes Housed in Data Centers
for Superior Infrastructure

By deploying our stake pools in data centers, we take advantage of high-bandwidth, redundant internet connectivity, state-of-the-art power supply systems, and other cutting-edge enterprise solutions. Each data center is staffed by dedicated technical professionals, safeguarding operational continuity 24/7.
