How to delegate to OCEAN stake pools with Ledger device Hardware Wallet.


1. Upgrade Cardano application on the Ledger device Hardware Wallet
1.1. Start “Ledger Live” application on your PC
1.2. Select “Manager” from the menu
1.3. Connect your Ledger device Hardware Wallet to PC
1.3.1. Follow the instructions displayed in the “Ledger Live” application to connect your Ledger device correctly.
1.3.2. Select “Apps installed”
1.3.3. Then select “Update all”
1.3.4. Confirm that Cardano application version is 2.0.3 or greater.
2. Open your Ledger device Hardware Wallet account with AdaLite
2.1. Navigate to https://adalite.io/ wallet
2.2. Select “Hardware wallet”
2.3. Select “Unlock with Ledger”
2.4. Confirm public key export on your Ledger device
3. Convert your Byron account to Shelley
3.1. Navigate to “Staking” section of AdaLite wallet
3.2. Your overall balance will be displayed with your “Non-staking” balance. As your wallet is from byron era, it needs to be converted to shelley wallet before staking can be possible
3.3. Select “Convert to stakable” to convert your Byron account to Shelley account
3.4. Then, confirm transaction by selecting “Confirm Transaction”
3.5. You will be presented with following screen, confirming your available “Staking balance”
4. Delegate to Stake Pool
4.1. Navigate to “Delegate Stake” section
4.2. Replace the “Stake Pool ID” field with one of the following OCEAN Stake pool identifiers:

4.4. Then, select “Delegate”
4.5. Confirm delegation transaction details as per following example
4.6. Confirm following details related to delegation on your Ledger device

“Start new transaction”

“Transaction fee...”

“Transaction TTL epoch ... /slot ...”

“Delegate stake to pool”

“Staking key ...”

“Confirm delegation”

4.7. If successful you will be presented with OCEAN Stake Pool in “Current delegation” section.
